# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

## Overview

Rsme is a local-first resume and cover letter builder. This policy explains how your data is handled when you use the app.

## Data Collection

Rsme does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All resume and cover letter data you create remains entirely on your device and is never sent to any external service except as described below.

## AI Processing

When you use AI-powered features (suggestions, ATS scoring, cover letter generation, job link analysis, resume parsing), the content you provide (resume text, job descriptions, job URLs) is sent to Groq's API via the Groq SDK. This data is processed temporarily to generate responses and is not stored by us. Groq's handling of that data is governed by their own privacy policy.

## File Uploads

If you upload a resume file (PDF, DOCX, TXT) for parsing, the file is sent to a local Express server running on your machine for text extraction. No file data is transmitted externally beyond your local environment.

## Local Storage

Rsme may use browser local storage to persist your session data. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

## Third-Party Services

- **Groq API** — Used for AI features. See [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy).
- **Google Fonts** — The Inter and Playfair Display fonts are loaded from Google Fonts, which may log your IP address.

## Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected here with an updated date.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue on the project repository.
